3. Budget Strategy

'Conversion' and 'Convergence' of 10% of Festival Budget

Use it not as a simple event cost, but as a residential program budget that makes the external population live for 2 weeks.

AS-IS (Current)

Limitations of Existing Festival Budget

  • One-time Expenditure: Concentration on volatile costs such as performance fees and stage installation costs
  • Short Stay: Performance viewing (2 hours) → 1 meal → Return home
  • Low Trickle-down Effect: Weak link to accommodation and multi-meal dining within the region
TO-BE (Proposal)

Budget Conversion to Educational Tourism

  • Stay-type Budget: Cost of attracting living population residing for more than 2 weeks
  • Festival Synergy: Utilizing student performance presentations as main festival content
  • Global Zone Operation: Native instructors operate English experience booths at the festival site
